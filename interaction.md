# Racing Game Interaction Design

## Core Game Mechanics

### Vehicle Control System
- **Mouse Control**: Click and drag to steer, scroll wheel for acceleration/braking
- **PS5 Controller**: Full analog stick steering, trigger buttons for acceleration/braking, button mapping for handbrake and boost
- **Keyboard Backup**: WASD or arrow keys for basic movement
- **Physics-Based Driving**: Realistic car handling with momentum, friction, and collision detection

### Destruction System
- **Dynamic Car Damage**: Visual damage models that affect performance
- **Environmental Destruction**: Smash through barriers, destroy props, create debris
- **Crash Physics**: Realistic collision effects with particle systems
- **Explosion Effects**: Dramatic car explosions with fire and smoke particles

### Scoring System
- **Race Points**: Earn points for race position, lap times, and stunts
- **Destruction Bonus**: Bonus points for destroying objects and causing chaos
- **Combo Multiplier**: Chain stunts and destruction for score multipliers
- **Leaderboard**: Persistent high score tracking

### Game Modes
- **Free Roam**: Explore the city without objectives
- **Race Mode**: Compete against AI opponents
- **Destruction Mode**: Cause maximum chaos for points
- **Time Trial**: Beat lap records

## Interactive Elements

### Vehicle Selection
- **Car Garage**: Choose from 8+ different vehicles with unique stats
- **Customization**: Paint jobs, performance upgrades, visual modifications
- **Stats Display**: Speed, acceleration, handling, durability ratings

### Environment Interaction
- **Traffic System**: Dynamic AI traffic that reacts to player
- **Destructible Objects**: Barrels, signs, buildings with damage states
- **Interactive Props**: Ramps, jumps, secret areas to discover
- **Weather Effects**: Rain, fog, night/day cycles affecting gameplay

### UI Elements
- **Speedometer**: Real-time speed display with boost indicator
- **Minimap**: Radar showing position, opponents, and objectives
- **Score Display**: Current score, combo counter, time remaining
- **Damage Indicator**: Visual car damage status and repair stations

### Audio System
- **Engine Sounds**: Dynamic engine audio based on RPM and damage
- **Collision Sounds**: Crashes, scrapes, explosions with positional audio
- **Ambient Soundscape**: City sounds, traffic, weather effects
- **Music System**: Dynamic soundtrack that intensifies with action

## Multiplayer Features
- **Local Split-Screen**: 2-4 player local multiplayer
- **Online Leaderboards**: Compare scores globally
- **Ghost Cars**: Race against best lap times
- **Challenge Mode**: Share custom challenges with friends

## Accessibility Options
- **Control Remapping**: Customizable controls for all input methods
- **Difficulty Settings**: Adjustable AI, damage, and physics
- **Visual Aids**: Optional racing lines, brake indicators
- **Audio Cues**: Screen reader support and audio feedback
